 Adidas US Sales Data Analysis

An exploratory data analysis of Adidas sales performance across the United States,
visualized using Tableau software.

Visualizations 

- Product Performance vs Average — Bar chart comparing category-wise sales against the overall average
- Total Units Sold by State — Choropleth map highlighting regional demand across the US
- Operating Profit by Retailer — Pie chart breaking down profit share across key retail partners
- Rise of Online Sales (2021 Inflection) — Line chart showing the consumer behavior shift,
  with 2021 marked as the clear inflection point where online sales surged past traditional channels

Key Insight

> 2021 was the turnning point — online sales channels saw a sharp upward inflection,
> outpacing in-store and other retail sales methods, reflecting a post-pandemic
> shift in consumer buying behavior.

 Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel / Tableau (data prep & charting)
- PowerPoint (dashboard presentation)
